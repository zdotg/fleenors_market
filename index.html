Fleenor's Market

Compact, community-focused, and rooted in Durham pride.

A cozy space where good wine, snacks, and neighborhood life meet. Inspired by European bodegas and the markets we've loved on our travels, we've reimagined the grab-and-go experience with a focus on warmth, quality, and everyday essentials done right.

You'll find rotating selections of natural wine, craft beer, and local beverages, alongside curated snacks, frozen favorites, and easy grab-and-go meals — everything you'd want for a picnic at the park, a night in, or an impromptu get-together.

Founded by Zach Gallman and Josh Fleenor, the shop reflects our combined backgrounds in hospitality, wine, and community building. After years in Asheville's food and beverage scene, we returned to Durham to create something that feels both local and lasting — a space that brings people together while honoring simple pleasures.

At just 418 square feet, Fleenor's proves that small can still be full of life. We're here to make the everyday feel special — one bottle, one snack, one neighbor at a time.
